Activated Carbon Powders For Paracetamol Industries

Black Diamond Carbon is a renowned manufacturer of Activated CarbonPowders for Paracetamol Industries in India. Manufacturing superior quality Chemically Activated Carbon using high standard German Technology. We use Pinewood Sawdust Base & Wood Charcoal Base or Coal Base to produce various grades of Chemically and Steam-Activated Carbons washed carbons. We also manufacture carbons custom made to consumers specifications.

We have a well-equipped, modern testing laboratory and each material is tested several times during intermediate stages of production and then before despatch. Testing procedures can be supplied on request.

We offer technical assistance to user, including suggestions about what grade to use for an application and also how to draw maximum benefit from the use of a carbon. In some cases where granular carbon is used in big tonnage, we can regenerate the spent material and return to the user to effect considerably savings.

As a chemist by qualification, he practices stringent production control to ensure superior quality at all stages of production.
